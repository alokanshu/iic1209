IIC Innovation Ambassador Training Series

Objective


Institution Innovation councils at institutes are envisioned an instrumental role to drive innovation and startup ecosystem at campus while connecting with ecosystem enablers at regional and national level. For this, the role of faculty being a member of IIC is very important. They play the role of a mentor or guide for young minds in their pursuit of Innovation and entrepreneurship and It is equally crucial for mentors to have the adequate and right set of mentoring skills. Considering the fact that Indian HEIs are lacking in innovation and start-up mentor capacity to guide students on IPR, Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Institutions Innovation council of MHRD’s Innovation Cell aims to build mentorship capacity of IIC members through a series of training programs exclusively for faculty members and the trained IIC members are designated as "IIC - Innovation Ambassador". IIC-MHRD's Innovation Cell has taken a target to train more than 5000 faculties to develop mentoring skills on above four themes by end of June 2020, the end of IIC Calendar.

The beneficiaries of these training programs will join the network as "IIC-Innovation Ambassador" and will perform the role of mentor in their respective IICs, these Innovation Ambassadors will provide support to other IICs as resource person in organizing various programs related to innovation and startup and spread the message of innovation among the students and faculties. As part of Incentive and recognition system of IIC, the brief profile of these Innovation Ambassadors will feature on IIC portal for public display.

Post training these Innovation Ambassadors are felicitated with a token of recognition and responsibility which contains the badge of Innovation Ambassador along with AICTE and MIC logo badge. This token of recognition will reminisce Innovation Ambassadors about the responsibilities entrusted with them by MIC & AICTE.

After completion of training these Innovation Ambassadors will have to complete various activities as prescribed and suggested by IIC coordination team at MIC. Innovation Ambassadors will be given activities at regular intervals to create awareness, inculcate entrepreneurial mindset and bring cognizance about startups, IPR and importance of Innovation ecosystem enablers and facilitators among students and fellow faculty members

About Ambassador Series

01.

Role of IIC’s Innovation Ambassador

Perform the role of mentor in their respective IICs
Provide support to other IICs as resource person
Spread the message of innovation & start-up among the students and faculties.

02.

Who can participate?

IICs have upgraded/completed their council as per the IIC2.0
IIC Faculty/Staff member nominated by IIC.
Flexible and volunteer to offer service.
Preference should be given to the members with prior experience in Entrepreneurship and Innovation.

03.

How to apply?

Login in IIC Portal.
Complete MY COUNCIL information.
Click on Innovation ambassador menu
Nominate one member for each training program.
