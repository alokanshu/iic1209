IIC 2019-20

IIC 2.0 (July 2019 – June 2020)

New IIC calendar plan with various new features has been introduced from July 2019. More than 500 new Institutes were approved to establish IICs at their campuses. To make comprehensive impact in field of innovation, IPRs and Entrepreneurship following measures were introduced in IIC2.0 Edition-

  • • Flexible Semester based IIC calendar plan for HEIs to plan and organize the activities in the campus.
  • • Introduction of IIC calendar Driven Activities, Self-Driven Activities and MIC Driven Activates with differential scoring mechanism to encourage healthy competition among HEIs.
  • • Provision of regular council meeting (Quarterly) to plan for various activities under IIC.
  • • New upgraded portal for report submission, Information dissemination and query resolution

IIC 2.0: Regional Mentoring sessions for POC students

MHRDs Innovation Cell has organised 14 orientation session during the period from 19 July to 19 August 2019 at 12 cities across India. Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII) Ahmedabad and Forge Incubator, Coimbatore were sourced as knowledge partner organisations to conduct these orientation sessions. The experts from these organisations and MHRDs Innovation Cell conducted a day long mentoring through various tools and techniques. The mentoring session was concluded with pitching round for selecting best POCs for National Level Bootcamp.
The purpose of these mentoring sessions were to mentor and guide students with their submitted POCs on various aspects of conceiving a startup. The experts also provided one on one guidance to students on how to carry forward their POCs to a startup level.

MORE FROM IIC 2.0

01.

Regional orientation and training sessions for faculties from IICs

Total 14 orientation session were organised during the period from 19 July to 19 August 2019. The purpose of these training sessions were to train IIC representative from Institutes about features of IIC portal system and scoring mechanism for doing various IIC activities. The sessions also focused on orientation of faculties regarding unique IIC modal and its approach to foster innovation at Institute level.

02.

Two- Day bootcamp on Business Plan & Enterprise Development for students

POC Teams selected from regional level pitching sessions, organised during regional mentoring sessions were further scrutinised by a panel at national level at AICTE HQ, New Delhi. Total 43 teams were selected and invited to attend the Two- Day bootcamp on Business Plan & Enterprise Development for students. Another 25 teams from Smart India Hackathon and Chatraviswakarma Award (organized by AICTE) were invited to participate in program at AICTE HQ, New Delhi on 9th and 10th Sept.2019. On 11th Sept. 2019 these teams demonstrated the prototype at MIC National exhibition at AICTE, New Delhi and 5 teams received MHRD Innovation Award 2019.

03.

Smart India Hackathon 2019

The third edition of SIH (SIH 2019) was organised last year and it saw a participation of 95+ industries, 18 ministries with around 55 MSMEs by submitting problem statements. More than 2 Lac students participated from all over the country to submit their ideas against the problem statements. Smart India Hackathon 2019 addressed both software and hardware challenges just like its previous edition in the year 2018. Crowd-sourcing of good ideas from young technical students for addressing the governance related challenges has already been proved to be extremely successful in previous editions.

04.

Innovation Day – Fortnight Celebration

Dr. Abdul Kalam once said that "Innovation is born out of the cultural excellence”, and to achieve this cultural excellence and shift in traditional culture of education, MHRD’s Innovation Cell is working diligently through its various initiatives. In same line of efforts MIC has asked all IICs to conduct various programs from 1st October 2019 till 15th October 2019 to create awareness about Innovation and entrepreneurship. Institutes conducted various activities like debates, writing competition, poster competitions, Innovation song creation related to Innovation and entrepreneurship, invited nearby school students to make them aware about Innovation and entrepreneurial infrastructure in their vicinity, award and felicitate faculty and student innovators/ entrepreneurs, various workshops, and exhibition etc.
