IIC 2018-19

IIC 1.0 (November 2018 – June 2019).

IIC program was inaugurated on 21st November 2018 at AICTE HQ New Delhi by Minister of HRD and a network of 950+ IICs were established across country. Through various activities driven by MHRD’s Innovation Cell through IICs at HEIs level has made an impact with reach out to more than 7.5 lakh students and 63,000+ faculties to receive exposure on various aspects of Innovation and start-up.


IIC 1.0: Star Achievers

During first year of formation of IICs, MHRD’s Innovation cell have prescribed an Annual Calendar plan for various activities related to Innovation, IPR, Leadership and Entrepreneurship. IICs participated with great enthusiasm in these activities and as provisioned, submitted report on IIC portal in prescribed format. For each activity scores were automatically generated based on the parameters informed to IICs before commencement of activities. These report and scores were further verified by IIC Coordination team. Based on these scores, associated with successful completion of each activity, star Rating for IICs were generated.
Top three IICs from each zone and total 25 IICs from across the country were announced on 11th Sept 2019 and were felicitated by Hon’ble Minister of HRD.

02.

Smart India Hackathon 2017 & 2018

Smart India Hackathonon 2017 and 2018 had received massive response from the students. 7531 ideas were received in SIH 2017 for 598 problem statements. The best ideas were awarded in the grand finale. Best ideas winning ideas were further developed using funding support from AICTE. More ideas were further developed and students were also offered internship opportunities by their problem statement creator Ministries or Industries. A total of 57+ projects have been dployed finally to the concerned minisries/departments. While SIH2017 was focused on sofwtare governace challenges faced by the different departments, SIH2018 had involved hardware challenges as well. 12047 ideas were received for 340 software challenges while more than 4300 ideas were received for around 50 hardware challenges. 18 software and 11 harwdware best winning ideas were selected for further development with funding supports

03.

Proof of Concept (POC) Contest

MIC has started a pan India contest for all the registered IICs in month of May- June 2019. About 600 Institutes participated in it and submitted more than 1700 POCs. Conducting field visits, identifying problem statements, scouting ideas from students on these problems, first level shortlisting at Institute level and submission of best ideas (maximum four) on IIC portal were Prerequisite for this contest. Final submitted ideas were mentored by MHRDs innovation Cell by conducting various regional mentoring and orientation sessions across country.

04.

Two-Day training program for faculties on “Mentorship Skill Development on Innovation and Entrepreneurship”

Based on the performance of IICs in various IIC activities given in IIC activity calendar 2018-19, IIC representatives from best performing 50 Institutes/IICs were invited to attend two-day training session at All India Council for technical Education Head Quarter New Delhi on 9th and 10th September 2019. Furthermore, top 25 IICs (Selected zone wise) were awarded by hon’able Minister, Human Resource Development Shri Ramesh pokhriyal ‘Nishankh’ for their active participation in IIC program during November 2018 to June 2019.
